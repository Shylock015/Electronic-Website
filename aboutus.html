Founded

Your trusted learning stationery and office accesories was founded in 2003 by Dr. Denis Mwanzia, initially a software development company. Over years the company has become a key and a dominant play in Africa Learning and Technology Sector. We are much known for our high quality, durable and price friendly products.

Expansion information

Shylock Enterprises was firstly expanded in 2006 for software company to both software and hardware company. Over years we have faced continous expansion due to increased demand of our products. We have expanded in both premises and technologywise. We are aiming for further expansion to cater for increased demand and to accommodate further technological advancement.
